UI/UX Design for Emergency Ambulance Finder Website

AmbuLink is a UI/UX website project created for a national web design competition. The project was designed to help users find nearby ambulance services, including both free and paid options, through a responsive, simple, and user-friendly interface. In this project, I focused on designing the UI/UX while ensuring the user flow remained clear and intuitive, allowing users to easily access information and navigate the platform efficiently, especially during emergency situations.
Fast nearby ambulance search
Free and paid ambulance service information
Responsive design with a simple and user-friendly interface
